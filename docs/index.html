Declutter. Earn. Reown.

Your pre-loved and unused items deserve a new life.

The Problem

Africans own valuable items such as clothes, electronics, accessories, and household goods that often sit unused. With no simple and trusted way to resell, these items gather dust, get discarded, or are given away. Existing platforms like Jiji and Facebook Marketplace are dominated by resellers, making it hard for everyday people to declutter, sell a few items, and earn with ease.

The Solution

Reown is a mobile first platform that makes reselling personal items effortless. In just a few steps, anyone can snap a photo, list an item, and connect with buyers. Designed for trust and simplicity, Reown helps people declutter, earn money, and keep goods in circulation, promoting both affordability and sustainability.
